NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT (SAMPLE)

This Non-Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of March 6, 2026,
by and between Contoso Legal Operations ("Disclosing Party") and Northwind Analytics
("Receiving Party"), in connection with evaluation of a potential vendor relationship.

1. Confidential Information
Confidential Information includes non-public legal strategy documents, draft contracts,
pricing schedules, data schemas, security architecture notes, and any client-identifying
information disclosed in written, oral, electronic, or visual form.

2. Permitted Use
Receiving Party may use Confidential Information solely to evaluate the proposed business
relationship and for no other purpose. Reverse engineering and model training use are prohibited.

3. Non-Disclosure Obligations
Receiving Party shall:
- keep Confidential Information strictly confidential;
- disclose only to personnel with a need to know and equivalent confidentiality obligations;
- apply no less than reasonable care, and at least the same care used for its own sensitive data.

4. Exclusions
Confidential Information does not include information that is:
(a) publicly available without breach of this Agreement;
(b) already known to Receiving Party without confidentiality restriction;
(c) independently developed without use of Confidential Information; or
(d) lawfully obtained from a third party without restriction.

5. Legal Compulsion
If disclosure is required by law or court order, Receiving Party must provide prompt notice
to Disclosing Party (to the extent legally permitted) and cooperate in seeking protective treatment.

6. Term and Survival
This Agreement remains in effect for two (2) years from the Effective Date.
Confidentiality obligations for trade secrets survive for as long as the information remains a trade secret.
All other confidentiality obligations survive for five (5) years after termination.

7. Return or Destruction
Upon written request, Receiving Party will return or securely destroy Confidential Information
within ten (10) business days, except archival backups retained under standard retention policy.

8. No License
No intellectual property rights are granted by this Agreement except the limited right to review
Confidential Information for the Permitted Use.

9. Governing Law
This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Washington, without regard to conflict-of-law principles.

END OF SAMPLE DOCUMENT
